Who We Are​

The FMIC is committed to offering high quality healthcare. 

Its vision is to become a tertiary medical centre of excellence and provide both health services and education in the quest to build a sustainable healthcare system for Afghanistan and the wider region.

Our Services​

The FMIC is a 176-bedded tertiary hospital based in Kabul.

We are providing world-class inpatient, outpatient, and diagnostic services to children, mothers, and adults in a variety of disciplines with the help of well-qualified doctors, nurses, and paramedics in a hygienic and friendly environment.​​

 Family Hifazat App Introduction & Activation 

1. What is Family Hifazat App? 

The Family Hifazat App will give you access to online applications where you can view health information for yourself and all your family members.

2. What are the Benefits of the Family Hifazat? ​​

With this unique platform, you and your family members can access individual medical profiles online from anywhere and anytime. 

• Diagnostic test results (Lab Test, Radiology Report)

• Record of previous and current medications

• Discharge summaries Your health is your biggest asset, therefore it needs attention and care. ​
